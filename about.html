About the platform

Selwa is designed to present Bhutanese craft with editorial calm, not marketplace noise.

For this milestone, the product is framed as a cultural marketplace: clear product browsing, strong artisan identity, and a visual system that feels respectful rather than generic.

The intention

Selwa exists to connect Bhutanese artisans with a wider audience while keeping the sense of origin intact. We wanted the platform to feel curated, tactile, and grounded in the rhythms of craft rather than the urgency of a discount-driven store.

  • Present product context before purchase pressure
  • Bring artisan names and regions forward in the interface
  • Use visual restraint so the objects feel dignified

The current milestone

Part B focuses on showcasing a believable user journey: home, catalogue, product detail, and account entry points. The frontend is intentionally static-friendly while remaining ready for later backend integration.
