Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about ordering, shipping, and working with Selwa.

Every product on Selwa is sourced directly from Bhutanese artisans. We visit workshops, verify materials, and work with each maker to set fair pricing. There are no middlemen or third-party resellers.
Orders over Nu. 5,000 ship free within Bhutan. International shipping is available at checkout. All craft pieces are carefully wrapped to protect fragile surfaces during transit. Delivery typically takes 5-10 business days.
We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unused items in their original packaging. Because each piece is handmade, slight variations in colour, texture, and dimensions are part of the craft and not considered defects.

If you are a Bhutanese artisan working in textiles, wood, metal, clay, or other heritage materials, you can apply through our contact page. We review applications and visit workshops before onboarding new makers to the platform.
