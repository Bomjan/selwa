Frequently asked questions

Find answers to the most common questions about ordering, shipping, and returns.

How do I place an order?
Browse our products, click "Add to cart", and head to your cart when you're ready. Follow the checkout steps to enter your shipping address and payment details. You'll receive a confirmation email once your order is placed.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
Orders can be modified or cancelled within 24 hours of placement. After that, the artisan will have begun preparing your item. Please contact us at support@selwa.bt as soon as possible if you need to make changes.
Will I receive an order confirmation?
Yes. You'll receive an email confirmation immediately after placing your order, and another notification with a tracking number once your item is dispatched by the artisan.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery typically takes 7–14 business days for international orders, and 3–5 business days within Bhutan. Handmade items may take a little longer as the artisan prepares your order with care. You'll always be notified of expected delivery times at checkout.
Is shipping free?
Yes — orders over Nu. 5,000 qualify for free shipping worldwide. For orders below that threshold, a flat shipping fee of Nu. 300 applies for domestic orders, and Nu. 800 for international orders.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 60 countries worldwide. At checkout, enter your country and the available shipping options and costs will be shown. Some remote locations may have extended delivery times.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a tracking number by email. You can use this to track your parcel on the Bhutan Post website or the international carrier's tracking page.
What is your return policy?
We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Because each product is handmade, minor natural variations in colour or texture are not grounds for return — but if your item arrives damaged or not as described, we'll make it right.
How do I start a return?
Email support@selwa.bt with your order number and reason for return. We'll send you a prepaid return label and instructions within 2 business days. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the returned item.
How much do artisans earn per sale?
Artisans keep at least 80% of every sale. The remaining amount covers platform maintenance, payment processing, and logistics support. Artisans always set their own prices, so there's no price manipulation from our side.
How can I become a Selwa artisan?
If you're a Bhutanese craftsperson, we'd love to hear from you! Visit our About page and fill in the contact form with subject "Artisan application". Our team reviews all applications within 7 business days. We look for quality, authenticity, and a connection to traditional Bhutanese craft.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, bank transfers, and mobile payment options. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption. We do not store your card details on our servers.
What currency are prices shown in?
Prices are listed in Bhutanese Ngultrum (Nu.). An approximate USD conversion is shown on product pages for reference. Your payment provider will apply the current exchange rate at the time of purchase.
Do you offer coupon codes or discounts?
Yes! We run seasonal promotions and occasionally share discount codes with our newsletter subscribers. You can enter a coupon code in your cart before checkout. Follow us on social media to stay updated on offers.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you within 2 business days.
