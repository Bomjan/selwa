Why we started

Bhutan's craft deserves the world's attention

Bhutan's artisans produce some of the world's most remarkable handcrafted goods — but for generations, they've had limited access to markets beyond their local communities. Selwa was founded in 2023 to change that.

We work directly with craftspeople across all 20 Dzongkhags, offering them a trusted platform, fair pricing, and the logistical support to reach customers around the world.

Every artisan on Selwa is verified, every product is authentic, and every purchase flows directly to the maker.

How it works

Direct from artisan to your door

When you buy on Selwa, your order goes directly to the artisan. They pack it with care — often with a handwritten note — and we handle the shipping and delivery.

No middlemen. No markups. At least 80% of every sale goes back to the maker. The remainder covers platform operations and the logistics of getting your item safely to your door.

You always know who made your product, where they live, and the craft tradition behind every piece.

What we stand for

Our principles

🤝
Fair trade

Artisans set their own prices and receive the majority of every sale — no price manipulation, ever.

🌿
Sustainability

We support natural materials, traditional dyes, and low-waste production methods rooted in Bhutanese tradition.

🏛️
Heritage

Every product preserves a craft tradition that spans generations — weaving, carving, pottery, and more.

💡
Transparency

You always know who made your product, where it came from, and exactly where your money goes.
