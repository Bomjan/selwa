About Selwa

A marketplace where craft, culture, and maker identity come first.

Selwa connects Bhutanese artisans with collectors and buyers who care about where things come from and how they are made.

Why Selwa exists

Bhutanese artisans produce some of the finest handmade textiles, woodcraft, and jewellery in South Asia, but most of it never reaches the right audience. Selwa bridges that gap by giving each maker a platform that respects their work.

  • Every product shows the artisan, region, and materials used
  • Artisans set their own prices with no middlemen
  • The collection is curated, not crowded

What we offer

Selwa is built around a browsing experience that lets the objects and their makers speak for themselves. We focus on quality over quantity, heritage materials over mass production, and cultural context over generic listings.
