Our story

Crafted with purpose

Selwa was born from a belief that the world deserves to experience the beauty of Bhutanese craftsmanship — and that artisans deserve fair recognition and income for their skill.

🧵

Why we started

Bhutan's artisans produce some of the world's most remarkable handcrafted goods — but for generations, they've had limited access to markets beyond their local communities. Selwa was founded in 2023 to change that.

We work directly with craftspeople across all 20 Dzongkhags, offering them a trusted platform, fair pricing, and the logistical support to reach customers around the world.

🌏

How it works

When you buy on Selwa, your order goes directly to the artisan. They pack it with care — often with a handwritten note — and we handle shipping and delivery. No middlemen, no markups.

At least 80% of every sale goes back to the maker. The rest covers platform costs and the logistics of getting your item safely to your door.

What we stand for

🤝
Fair trade

Artisans set their own prices and receive the majority of every sale.

🌿
Sustainability

We support natural materials, traditional dyes, and low-waste production methods.

🏛️
Heritage

Every product preserves a craft tradition that spans generations.

💡
Transparency

You always know who made your product and where your money goes.
