Privacy policy

How Selwa collects, uses, and protects your personal information. Last updated April 2026.

What we collect

When you use Selwa, we collect limited information to provide our services, process orders, and improve your experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Your rights

  • You may request a copy of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • We do not process payments directly; payment data is handled by our third-party payment provider.

Cookies and local storage

Selwa uses browser local storage to maintain your cart between visits. We use essential cookies for authentication. No third-party tracking cookies are used.
