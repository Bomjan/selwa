Summary Selwa collects only the data needed to connect you with Bhutanese artisans and fulfil your orders. We do not sell your personal information. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Section 01

Who We Are

Selwa ("we", "us", "our") is an online marketplace that connects customers worldwide with traditional Bhutanese artisans. Our platform allows artisans to list handcrafted products and buyers to purchase them directly, preserving centuries-old Bhutanese craftsmanship traditions.

Our registered address is: Selwa Marketplace, Thimphu, Bhutan. For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at privacy@selwa.bt.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, as well as data generated automatically when you use our platform.

Category Examples Source
Account data Name, email address, password (hashed) You
Order data Shipping address, order history, payment confirmation You / Payment provider
Artisan profile Bio, location (dzongkhag), craft speciality, product images Artisan
Usage data Pages visited, search terms, device type, IP address Automatically collected
Communications Messages sent to artisans or support You

We do not store full payment card details. Payments are processed securely by our third-party provider and only a transaction reference is retained.

Section 03

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal information only for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your Selwa account
  • To process orders and arrange delivery of products
  • To connect buyers with artisans and facilitate communication
  • To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and support responses
  • To improve and personalise your experience on the platform
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud or abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations under Bhutanese law
  • To send marketing emails — only with your explicit consent, which you may withdraw at any time

Section 04

Sharing Your Data

We share your personal data only where necessary:

  • Artisans: Your name and shipping address are shared with the artisan who fulfils your order.
  • Delivery partners: Courier and postal services receive your delivery address to fulfil shipments.
  • Payment processors: Our payment gateway receives your billing information to complete transactions.
  • Service providers: Hosting, analytics, and email services that operate under strict data processing agreements.
  • Legal authorities: Where required by Bhutanese law or in response to a valid legal request.

We never sell your personal data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Section 05

Cookies

Selwa uses cookies to ensure the platform functions correctly and to understand how visitors use the site. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for login, cart functionality, and security. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand page performance and user journeys (e.g. Google Analytics, anonymised). You can opt out via your browser settings.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language and currency settings.

You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to shop on Selwa.

Section 06

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Account data: Held until you request deletion or after 3 years of account inactivity.
  • Order records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial and tax regulations.
  • Usage / analytics data: Anonymised and aggregated after 26 months.
  • Marketing preferences: Until you unsubscribe or withdraw consent.

Section 07

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw marketing consent at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@selwa.bt. We will respond within 30 days.

Section 08

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers
  • Bcrypt hashing for stored passwords
  • Role-based access controls limiting staff access to personal data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please use a strong, unique password for your Selwa account.

Section 09

Children's Privacy

Selwa is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us at privacy@selwa.bt and we will delete it promptly.

Section 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page
  • Send a notification to registered users via email
  • Display a banner on the Selwa homepage for 30 days

Continued use of Selwa after any update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
