The Kishuthara is one of Bhutan's most celebrated traditional silk fabrics, woven by skilled artisans in the eastern districts. Each piece is hand-loomed using techniques passed down through generations, featuring intricate geometric patterns that carry deep cultural significance.

This fabric by Karma Wangmo uses naturally dyed silk threads in rich earth tones — saffron, crimson and indigo — creating a vibrant yet harmonious pattern. No two pieces are identical.

Suitable for traditional Bhutanese garments, home décor, or as a collector's piece. Hand wash recommended in cold water.
