Terms and expectations

Terms for interacting with the current Selwa prototype.

These terms are intentionally scoped to a class project milestone. They help the experience feel complete without pretending the current demo is a full production marketplace.

Using the prototype

Selwa is currently a presentation-ready concept product. Catalogue browsing, sign-in, sign-up, and cart interaction are included to demonstrate user experience, interface consistency, and backend planning for Part B.

Current experience boundaries

  • No live payments are processed in the current prototype.
  • Some user flows may be simulated when backend services are unavailable.
  • Product availability, pricing, and shipping details are representative for demonstration.

Presentation integrity

The design system, content structure, and interaction patterns are intended to reflect a realistic marketplace direction. Future milestones may expand terms around payments, fulfilment, refunds, and artisan agreements as the backend grows.

Prototype status

This site is strong enough for presentation and interaction testing, but it should still be understood as a milestone build rather than a launched commerce platform.
